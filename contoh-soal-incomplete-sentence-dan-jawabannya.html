8. incomplete adjective clauses


Jawaban:

An adjective clause—also called an adjectival or relative clause—will meet three requirements:

First, it will contain a subject and verb.

Next, it will begin with a relative pronoun [who, whom, whose, that, or which] or a relative adverb [when, where, or why].

Finally, it will function as an adjective, answering the questions What kind? How many? or Which one?

27. Incomplete Sentence 6 ) …….. clear and concise tender presentation, this company is appointed by local government to renovate the city hall a) Since b) Unless c) Even if d) Because of


Jawaban:

D. Because of

Penjelasan:

Because of merupakan kata yang menjelaskan alasan atau yang dalam bahasa Indonesia berarti 'oleh karena' atau 'karena'. Pada soal ini, alasan nya adalah clear and concise tender presentation.

Because of clear and concise tender presentation, this company is appointed by local government to renovate the city hall.

Karena penyajian yang jelas dan ringkas, perusahaan ini ditunjuk oleh pemerintah daerah untuk merenovasi balai kota.
