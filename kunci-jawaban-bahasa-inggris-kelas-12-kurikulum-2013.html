11. jawaban bahasa inggris kelas 9 kurikulum 2013 hal 13 dann 14


Jawaban buku Bahasa Inggris kelas 9 kurikulum 2013 halaman 13 dan 14 adalah tentang melengkapi dialog dengan complimenting and congratulating expression. Untuk melengkap dialog tersebut, kita harus memperhatikan situasi yang diberikan.

Pembahasan

Situation 2:

Lina has just finished drawing a picture of Mrs Ani , her english teacher. She will present it to her on her birthday. Siti praises the picture and she's sure that Mrs. Ani will like it.

Siti: “Lina, that is a beautiful picture. I am sure Mrs. Ani will like it.”

Beni:"Thanks. I hope so too."

Situation 3:

Udin has just finished making fried tofu for his friends. Dayu says that it looks nice and she’s sure that everybody will like it.

Dayu: “Great, your fried tofu looks nice. I am sure everybody will like it.”

Siti: “Thank you. I hope so too.”

Situation 4:

Beni has just finished mopping the floor of the health unit. Mr. Dani praises

him. He also thanks him for that.

Mr. Dani: “Good on you Beni. The floor of the health unit is clean now! Thanks.”

Beni: “You are welcome.”

Situation 5:

Siti has just finished practicing singing. She will sing in the celebration of the Kartini Day. Dayu praises her that she sings like a real singer, and she will get a big applause for that.

Siti: “You sing like a real singer. I’m sure you will get a very big applause.” 

Dayu: “Thank you. That’s very nice of you.”

Jadi, kalimat pertama pada situasi 2, 3 dan 5 adalah kalimat pujian (complimenting expression) yang kemudian diikuti dengan kalimat pengharapan (hoping expression) dengan menggunakan simple future tense (subject + will + verb 1).

Sedangkan pada situasi 4, kalimat pertama adalah complimenting expression yang kemudian diikuti dengan kalimat berterima kasih (thanking expression).

Based on the text, it is talking about the last school vacation and we will complete the text with the form of past tense.

The simple past tense, sometimes called the preterite, is used to talk about a completed action in a time before now. The time of the action can be in the recent past or the distant past and action duration is not important.

We always use the simple past when we say when something happened, so it is associated with certain past time expressions.

Simple Past Tense VS Past Continuous Tense

Simple Past adalah tense yang dipakai saat kejadian tersebut dimulai dan diakhiri di masa lampau. Juga dipakai saat menjadi kejadian kedua yang terjadi "mengganggu" saat kejadian pertama sedang berlangsung di masa lampau.

Past Continuous adalah tense yang dipakai saat menyatakan kegiatan tersebut sedang dilakukan atau kegiatan tersebut butuh waktu untuk melakukannya (proses) di masa lampau.

Contoh : I was watching TV, when you called me.

Kejadian pertama adalah menonton TV, lalu datang kejadian kedua yaitu menelepon.

I was writing a letter, while my sister was sewing her cloth.

Dua kejadian terjadi bersamaan di masa lampau dan dua-duanya membutuhkan waktu (proses).

I dropped my phone accidentally, when my dad came.

Dua kejadian terjadi bersamaan di masa lampau dan keduanya tidak membutuhkan proses (langsung terjadi begitu saja).

I will lead you to re!ect on what  you are learning now. Complete  the statements in the box. Discuss  it in your group and with me.

Now I know it is important ...

to say “CONGRATULATION” to people for their success, their  achievements, and their good fortunes.

to say “HOPE” to pray for other people’s success,  achievements, and good fortunes.

Pembahasan

Congratulation adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberikan ucapan selamat kepada orang lain atas pencapaian yang telah diraihnya.

Beberapa contoh ucapan congratulation adalah:

"Congratulations for winning basket competition” “This calls for celebrating! Congratulations!” “You did it! So proud of you!” “I knew it was only a matter of time. Well done!” “You’ve worked so hard for this. Congrats!” “This is awesome! You’re awesome! Way to go!” “Sincere congratulations on your hard-earned success.”

Hope adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mendoakan orang lain agar selalu sukses, beruntung, dan mendapatkan pencapaian yang bagus. Contoh kalimat hope adalah:

I hope I can see you soon.I hope you will get a better life.I hope you can be a succesfull person.I hope you become a ne president.I hope you always be lucky.I hope you get well soon
