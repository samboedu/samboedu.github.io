Son: Bagaimana dengan bangunan disebelahnya bank?

Father: Itu kantor pos.

Son: Jadi, bangunan disebelahnya bank adalah kantor pos.

Father: Betul. Bank dan kantor pos bersebelahan.

Father: Ingat. Kantor pajak berada di seberang bank dan kantor pos.

Son: Dan, bangunan apa yang ada di depan kita?

Father: Ini rumah sakit

Son: Oh ya... Bangunan itu rumah sakit?

Father: Betul. Bangunan yang ada dihadapan kita ini adalah rumah sakit

Father: Itu adalah sekolah

Father: Itu adalah kantor polisi

Jawaban bahasa Inggris kelas 9 halaman 136 adalah

(1) asked

(2) heart

(3) woods

(4) deer

(5) disappoint

(6) brought

(7) gave

(8) love

(9) knew

(10) deer's

(11) at

(12) a piece of

(13) told

(14) Badly

(15) village

Pembahasan

When he was twelve years old, Dayang Sumbi asked Sangkuriang to bring her deer's heart. But after many days in the woods, he could not find a deer. He did not want to disappoint his mother, so he killed Tumang and brought his heart home and gave it to his mother. Because of her love to Tumang, Dayang Sumbi knew, it was his heart not a deer's heart. She got very angry at Sangkuriang. She hit him with a piece of wood on his forehead and told him to go away. Badly wounded, Sangkuriang left her and the village.
