For example:

- May I help you?

2. Would you like me to + (action)?

For example:

- Would you like me to heat the food?

- Would you like me to prepare a cup of tea?

- Would you like me to bring your breakfast to your room?

Both patterns are very polite and often used in a business setting or for people working in tourism industry. Other patterns below are not as polite as the two patterns above.

3. Can I + (action)?

For example:

- Can I help you?

- Can I open the door?

4. Do you want me + (action)?

- Do you want me to buy coffee for you?

- Do you want me to include your name on the list?

- Do you want me to reserve the hotel for you?

The examples above are some expressions used in English to offer help/service. There are many others similar expression that can be used because there are no strict rules and to be able to use the expression properly, we have to practice several times and get used to it.

11. contoh dialog imperative 3 orang (bahasa inggris)


IMPERATIVE SENTENCES

Jawaban:

Contoh dialog imperative 3 orang:

Situation: This dialog between a mother and her two daughters Ann and Joy. They are in the kitchen, suddenly the Mom remembers that she runs out some ingredients that she needs to go to the shop nearby. The bold sentences are imperative sentences.

Mother: Ann, can you come to the kitchen for a moment?

Ann: Yes, sure Mom! (while she's walking to the kitchen)

Mother: Can you help me stir this soup? I need to check the bread in the oven.

Ann: Sure Mom! I'll help you.

Mother: Ooh...I forgot! I run out of some ingredients, I'll go to the shop across the road. And Ann, don't forget to keep stirring!

Suddenly, Joy is coming to the kitchen

Joy: Mom, I heard that you are going to the shop, Can I come too? I need to buy some crayons for my drawing.

Mother: Yes, but first get me my wallet, please. It's on the cupboard near the TV.

Joy: Here your wallet, Mom!

Mother: Put on your sandals, Joy! Ann, I won't be long!

Ann: No problem, don't forget to close the door!

Penjelasan:

IMPERATIVE SENTENCES

Imperative sentences adalah kalimat yang isinya memberikan perintah atau instruksi kepada seseorang untuk melakukan sesuatu.

Beberapa phrase yang sering digunakan dalam imperative sentences misalnya:

Be nice to your friends!Meet me at the city hall tomorrow at five!Close the window please.Enjoy your vacation!Don't forget to send me a letter when you get there!Please lower your voice!Open your book and write down your name!Please be quiet, the baby is sleepingHave a good time at the festival!Don't walk on the grass!

Penjelasan:

THERE

Kata 'there' digunakan untuk menunjukkan keberadaan sesuatu. Dalam terjemahan bebas bahasa Indonesia there berarti ada. Dalam penggunaannya 'there is' dan 'there are' ditentukan oleh berapa banyak benda (noun) yang disebutkan.

There is digunakan jika benda yang disebutkan hanya satu (tunggal)

There are digunakan jika benda yang disebutkan lebih dari satu (jamak)

Expressing of Checking Someone's Understanding

Phrase yang sering digunakan antara lain:

Do you understand?

Do you understand what I mean?

Are you with me?

Do you know what I'm saying?

Expression of Asking for Attention

Phrase yang sering digunakan antara lain

Attention, please!

May I have your attention, please?

Excuse me...

Stop talking please!

Can I have your attention, please?

Expression of Congratulation

Expression of congratulation adalah ekspresi yang biasa kita ucapkan ketika seseorang meraih keberhaslan atau mencapai sukses dalam meraih sesuatu atau melakukan sesuatu. Phrase yang sering digunakan antara lain:

Congratulation!

Happy Birthday!

Best Wishes!

Happy wedding!

Happy anniversary
