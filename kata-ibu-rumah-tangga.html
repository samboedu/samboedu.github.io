Langkah ² cara permutasi"

• YANG

= 4.3.2.1!

= 4×3×2×1

= 24 susunan kata

• SABAR

S=1

A=2

B=1

R=1

Total huruf = 5!

Unsur ganda = 2!

P = 5.4.3.2.1!/2.1!

= 5×4×3×2×1/2×1

= 120/2

= 60 susunan kata

• KAK

A=1

Total huruf = 3!

Unsur ganda = 2!

P = 3.2.1!/2.1!

= 3×2×1/2×1

= 6/2

= 3 susunan kata
