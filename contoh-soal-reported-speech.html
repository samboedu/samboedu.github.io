3. Contoh reported speech


Reported Speech
1. My father said that he was crazy.
2. She said to me that I walked down the hall.
3. Lionel reported to me that the soldiers was killed by the gun.reported speech, dalam bahasa Indonesia artinya adalah kalimat tidak langsung. perubahan kalimat dari direct speech menjadi reported speech memiliki beberapa aturan.

ini hanya beberapa contoh
1) imperative speech
My father said, "don't open that window!"
my father said not to open that window.
2) present tense
he said, "he is a student."
he said (that) he was a student.
tetapi karena kalimat ini berbentuk kebenaran umum, lebih pas 
he said (that) he is a student
3) past tense
my mother said, "You sister did her homework last night."
my mother said (that) my sister had done her homework previous night.

8. Apa itu 'reported thought' dan apa perbedaan nya dengan 'reported speech(direct&indirect). Tolong berikan contoh reported thought. Thanks


Sama saja sebenarnya. Reported speech melaporkan apa yang dikatakan sedangkan reported thought melaporkan kesimpulan.

Perbedaan mungkin ada di reporting verbnya. Reported speech menggunakan reporting verb seperti say, tell, atau ask dan yang lainnya.

Reported thought menggunakan reporting verb seperti wonder, know, persuade, dll.

Contoh reported thought :
direct : She said, "I'm late, sorry." -> kalau reported speechnya She said that she was late and she was sorry.

Tapi kita bisa simpulkan seperti ini: She apologized for being late. (pada kalimat langsungnya tidak ada kata apologized.






9. Pengertian reported speech dalam bahasa inggris beserta contohnya


Reported speech/ Indirect speech adalah cara menyampaikan perkataan yang telah diucapkan orang lain.

Contoh:

 1. dengan present simple dan present continous tense.
     "I eat a lot". He said He ate a lot (reported speech)
      " Riri is sleeping".  He said Riri was sleeping. (reported speech)
 2. Past simple and past continous tense.
      " I lived in Bandung". She told me that she had lived in Bandung.
      "I was living in Bali. She told me that she had been living in Bali.
3. Perfect tense
     " You have been so nice to me". She said that he'd been so nice to her (reported speech)

29. perbedaan direct speech dan reported speech


Direct speech (kalimat langsung), biasanya ditandai dengan tanda kutip (sama spt dlm penggunaan dlm bhs Indonesia). Reported speech, disebut juga indirect speech (kalimat tdk langsung). Perbedaan mendasar hny terletak pd tanda kutip. Penggunaan direct ke indirect, dlm bhs inggris mengalami perubahan tense. Contoh: bila simple present tense mjd past tense. (D = he said, "i always wake up early." > he said that he always woke up early)Direct speech : kalimat langsung
Indirect speech : Kalimat tidak langsung
