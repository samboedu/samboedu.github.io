Nice to know you that you come from a long distance country, Indonesia. A tropical country, indeed, I wish I shall visit you someday.

North Pole where I live now is quite inhospitable place for you, Tri. It is too cold for people from tropics. You have sunshine for 7 days while the sun rarely show up here.

Keep in touch, pal.

Yours truly,

Katyusha
________
katyusha_arsenal@yeeha.com

Selalu kontak, ya.

Salam,

20. Contoh surat undangan non formal beserta balasannya dalam bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

Surat kiriman:

Dear Mrs. Tailor

My name is Ria. I have big problem. My mom always get angry with me when she knows that I play games in the afternoon. You know, I like games very much but she doesn’t understand me. She lets me play games only on Sunday. It’s frustrating.

Riani

(Kepada Ibu Tailor

Nama saya adalah Ria. saya memiliki masalah besar. Ibu saya selalu marah kepada saya saat dia tahu bahwa saya bermain game pada siang hari. Kamu tahu, saya sangat menyukai game tapi ibu tidah pernah mengerti saya. Ibu saya meembiarkan saya bermain game hanya pada hari minggu. Ini membuaatku frustasi.

Riani)

Surat balasan:

I’m sure our parents love us. They want us to be the best. I know that you like game but you should manage your time well. You shouldn’t play games every day. You’ll get tired of them. You should show your time table to your parents and prove that you keep it well.

Regards

Mrs. Tailor

(Saya yakin bahwa orangtua kita menyayangi kita. Mereka menginginkan yang terbaik untuk kita. saya mengerti bahwa kamu menyukai game namun kamu harus mengatur waktumu dengan baik. Kamu tidak seharussnya bermain game setiap hari. Kamu akan lelah pada mereka. Kamu harus menunjukan tabel waktumu pada orangtuamu dan meenunjukan bahwa kamu menjaganya dengan baik.

Hormat,

Ibu Tailor)

Penjelasan:

semoga bermanfaat

27. contoh surat pribadi bhs inggris berserta balasannya


23 St. Mary Road,
Dublin 4, Ireland
Monday, 13th November

Dear Rieka,
How are you? I'm very well here. I came to Dublin a few months ago, and I told you I'll be here for work, but I ended up in university again, studying Language.
The university I'm going to right now is an international language university, and I'm taking English for the course.
I'm staying with my friend Katrina, and her parents. Mr and Mrs Kendall are really kind. They helped me in the first few days of me being in Ireland.
Here in Ireland, there are lots and lots of art galleries. I'm sure you are going to love them! There are also lots of sightseeing areas.
So, I think I'll write till here, since my homeworks are over loading, and reply soon!
I'd love to hear from you.
Write to me soon.
Love, Misa.

Balasan:

39 Rose Road,
London, England
Thursday, 23rd November

Dear Misa,
it's been a while! I'm doing fine thank you! I heard about you moving to Dublin a few weeks before you moved from your parents. Oh! and I need to thank your mother for the amazingly delicious cookies she made! I appreciate them very much!
Apparently, Katrina Kendall, your friend, is my sister's colleague in her office. I believe she's 26? I heard she also has a boyfriend and they recently just got engaged!
Mr and Mrs Kendall are actually my parent's friends. So we have a good relationship with them. We contact them at least twice a month.
And yes, I've heard of the art galleries there. Personally, I want to check out Guertena's works of art, and the sculptures too. I don't particularly like sightseeing, but I would like to give this one a try.
I understand that this letter is shorter, but considering my terrible writer's block, this is the most I can do. I'm planning to go and visit you sometime in the weekend.
Looking forward to see you!
Reply Soon!
Love, Rieka.

My favorite subject is Math. What is your favorite subject ?

I live in a small island called Bawean, Indonesia. It is very beautiful island. Have you ever heard about Indonesia?

I know little bit about your country. It’s snowy right now, isn’t it? I wonder of how exciting of making a snowman!

Unfortunately, We’ve got no snowyseason. I also wonder what you do in the snowy season.

I believe you’ve got a lot of interesting activities to do. I hope you enjoy your day. I’ll be waiting your reply.

 

Sincerely

Via
