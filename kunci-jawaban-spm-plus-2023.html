Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

L1(Persegi panjang)=P×L

=48cm×25cm

=1200cm²

L2

a=48cm

b=40cm

t=50cm-25

=25

L2(Trapesium)=½×(a+b)×t

[tex] = \frac{1}{2} \times (48 + 40) \times 25 \\ = \frac{1}{2} \times 88 \times 25 \\ = \frac{1}{2} \times 2200 \\ = 1100 {cm}^{2} [/tex]

Luas bangun gabungan=L1+L2

=1200cm² + 1100cm²

=2300cm²
