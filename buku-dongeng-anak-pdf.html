banyak buku teknologi?

= 100% - (3/8 + 22,5% + 0,25)

[tex] = \frac{40}{40} - ( \frac{3}{8} + \frac{1}{4} + \frac{ \frac{45}{2} }{100} )[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{40}{40} - ( \frac{15}{40} + \frac{10}{40} + \frac{9}{40} )[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{40}{40} - \frac{34}{40} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{6}{40} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{3}{20} [/tex]

buku dongeng

[tex] = \frac{15}{40} [/tex]

[tex] = = 22.5\%[/tex]
