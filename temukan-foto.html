9. temukan derajat QPS dan RQS link foto di bawah


Jawab:

∠RQS = 26°

∠QPS = 134°

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

RQS = RSQ

Segitiga sama kaki, 2 sudut kaki sama besar

Jumlah sudut segitiga 180

Sudut berhadapan sama besar : QPS = QRS

RQS = (180-134) /2

        =46/2 = 26°

QPS = 134°

18. temukan keliling dari diameter yang di berikan di foto​


Rumus Keliling Lingkaran = π x d

1).Diameter = 7cm

Keliling = 22/7 x 7

Keliling = 22cm

2).Diameter = 20cm

Keliling = 3,14 x 20

Keliling = 62,8cm

3).Diameter = 28cm

Keliling = 22/7 x 28

Keliling = 88cm

4).Diameter = 35cm

Keliling = 22/7 x 35

Keliling = 110cm

5).Diameter = 50cm

Keliling = 3,14 x 50

Keliling = 157cm

Notes:

•Penggunaan Phi (π), 22/7 untuk diameter yang bisa dibagi dengan 7,

•Sedangkan 3,14 untuk diameter yang tidak bisa dibagi dengan 7

Expression of Asking repetition adalah ekspresi yang digunakan ketika kita meminta seseorang mengulang apa yang diucapkan karena kita tidak mendengar atau tidak yakin apa yang kita dengar.

Phrase yang sering digunakan dalah expression of asking repetition antara lain adalah :

Excuse me?Pardon me?Can you repeat what you said?Can you repeat that please?Could you speak a little louder?Could you say that again?Sorry, I didn't catch that.Sorry, i didn't hear you.What did you say?What was that again?Pembahasan

Complete the dialogue below :

1. Parking Boy: You are fined by Rp. 5000, 00 Sir.

  Mr. Josh: What did you say? (1)

  Parking Boy: You have to pay Rp. 5000, 00 because you lost the parking card, Sir.

  Mr. Josh: Oh, really? Sorry, boy. Here you are.

  Parking Boy: Thank you, Sir

2. Teacher: We have to read the headline of a newspaper if we want to know the hot news today.

   Student: Sorry Ma'am, could you please repeat what you said? (2)

   Teacher: You have to read the headline of a newspaper of you want to know the latest news.

   Student: Oh, I see. Thank you Ma'am

3. Lia: What television program did you watch last night?

   Ade: Sorry?

   Lia: I want to know what television program you watched last night.

   Ade: Oh, It was the National geographic program (3)

   Secretary: Pardon me, Sir?

   Secretary: I had typed it and completed it yesterday?
