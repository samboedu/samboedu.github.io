Pembahasan

f(x) = (1 – x)² (2x + 3)

f'(x) = (1 – x) (1 – x) (2x + 3)

f'(x) = (1 – 2x + x²) (2x + 3)

f'(x) = (x² – 2x + 1) (2x + 3)

f'(x) = x² (2x + 3) – 2x (2x + 3) + 1 (2x + 3)

f'(x) = 2x³ + 3x² – 4x² – 6x + 2x + 3

f'(x) = 2x³ – x² – 4x + 3

f'(x) = 6x² – 2x – 4

f'(x) = 2 (x – 1) (3x + 2)✅
