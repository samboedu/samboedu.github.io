Jawaban:

Present Continous tense

artinya waktu yang sedang terjadi atau berlangsung

Rumus :

(+) S + Tobe + Ing + O + K.t.w

contoh : 1. We are studying english now

2. Mr. Akri is walking to the office

(-) S + Tobe + Not + V1 + Ing + O + K. w. t

contoh : 1. My Brother is not picking up flowers

2. I am not teaching english at 9A Class

(?) Tobe + S + V1 + Ing + O + K. w. t

contoh : 1. Are you teaching english at 9A Class ?

2. Are the thieves running away?
