Jawaban:

1. Their bikes was being repaired by me.

2. The dish was not being eaten by them.

3. The gate was not being painted by us.

4. This issue was being discussed by us.

5. The guitar was being played by him melodiously.

6. A film was being watched by her.

7. This letter had been rewritten by me.

8. My work had been finished by me.

9. The city had been defended by the brave men.

10. The fire had been put out by firefighters succesfully.

Penjelasan :

PAST CONTINUOUS TENSE

Active Voice : Subject + was/were + V2 + ing + Object.

Passive Voice : Object + was being/were being + V3 + by Subject.

(Kalimat yang menggunakan Past Continuous Tense adalah Kalimat nomor 1-6)

PAST PERFECT TENSE

Active Voice : Subject + had + V3 + Object.

Passive Voice : Object + had been + V3 + by Subject

(Kalimat yang menggunakan Past Perfect Tense adalah kalimat nomor 7-10)

Discussion:

Passive voice merupakan kalimat yang disampaikan secara inaktif dengan mematikan peranan aktif subyek atau obyek sebagai pemeran utama pada kalimatnya. Kalimat pasif diawali oleh obyek. Peranan obyek pada pekerjaan/kegiatan pada kalimat diperlakukan oleh subyek.

Ada ciri-ciri pada kalimat pasif:

menggunakan past participlemenyesuaikan to be yang digunakan berdasarkan tense-nyasaling memindahkan posisi subyek dan obyek

Explanation:

Hello! Allow me to explain your question about passive voice.

To change the sentences, don't forget to pay attention to tense it used.

Perhatikan tense yang digunakan sebelum menggatikan dan ingat polanya.

-number 1-

menggunakan present continuous karena terdapat penggunaan to be + V-ing dengan be adalah is.

untuk merubahnya, pindahkan sufiks -ing ke letak be setelah menggunakan to be dengan V3O + to be (is/am/are) + being + V3 + (by — S) + ...

memilih to be dengan menyesuaikan obyeknya

is: he, she, it, name, objectam: Iare: you, they, we, names, objects

dengan O adalah his materials berupa objects jadi to be: is tidak berlaku dan menggantikannya dengan are.

-number 2-

menggunakan present perfect dengan penggunaan to has + V3 dengan has: has

untuk merubahnya, gunakan be untuk perfect form: been di posisi di antaranya to has dengan V3O + has/have + been + V3 + (by — S) + ...

memilih has/have berdasarkan obyeknya

has: he, she, it, name, objecthave: I, you, they, we, names, objects

-number 3-

menggunakan simple present karena tidak ada pembentukan tambahan dengan V1(-s/es) menyesuaikan subyeknya.

untuk merubahnya, gunakan be untuk present form: is/am/are dengan V1 -> V3O + to be (is/am/are) + V3 + (by — S) + ...

-number 4-

merupakan kalimat yang menyatakan pekerjaan sebelumnya secara sederhana merupakan bentuk simple past dengan penggunaan V2.

untuk merubahnya, gunakan be untuk past form: was/were dengan V2 -> V3O + to be (was/were) + V3 + (by — S) + ...

pilih was/were menyesuaikan dari obyeknya

was: I, he, she, it, name, objectwere: you, they, we, names, objects

the bank robber dianggap sebagai object atau benda tunggal; menggunakan was

-number 5-

merupakan pernyataan yang akan dilakukan nanti belum tentu (secara sederhana disampaikan) merupakan bentuk simple future dengan penggunaan will.

untuk merubahnya, tambahkan be setelah will dengan V1 -> V3O + will + be + V3 + (by — S) + ...

—

*note: pada kalimat pasif, diperbolehkan untuk menyembunyikan atau menghilangkan subyek, sebagai contoh:

his materials are being prepared for the meeting.the bank robber was arrested.

10. change the following active sentence into passive voice!​


Active and Passive VoiceHow to change all tenses to Passive Voice??

Berikut adalah rumus inti dari semua bentuk passive voice. Perhatikan bahwa bentuk dasar semua tenses yang diubah ke Passive Voice adalah BE + V3.

-------------- ACTIVE -----------> PASSIVE

PRESENT

I paint the wall -----> The wall is painted by meI am painting the wall -----> The wall is being painted by meI have painted the wall -----> The wall has been painted by me  I have been painting the wall -----> The wall has been being  painted by me

PAST

A thief stole my wallet -----> My wallet was stolen by a thiefA thief was stealing my wallet -----> My wallet was being stolen by a thiefA thief had stolen my wallet -----> My wallet had been stolen by a thiefThe police had been watching a fugitive -----> A fugitive had been being watched by the police.

FUTURE

He will drive the car -----> The car will be driven by himHe will be driving the car -----> The car will be being driven by himHe will have driven the car -----> The car will have been driven by himHe will have been driving the car -----> The car will have been being driven by him

MODAL

They must keep the secret -----> The secret must be kept by themThey can keep the secret -----> The secret can be kept by themThey may keep the secret -----> The secret may be kept by themThey shall keep the secret -----> The secret shall be kept by themThey should keep the secret -----> The secret should be kept by them

Ingat!! Pelaku atau subjek dari kalimat pasif bisa dihilangkan.

Contoh:

Someone stole my wallet --- > My wallet was stolen.

PASSIVE: The room is being decorated by the boys at the moment.

3. The author has written a novel about teens life.

PASSIVE: A novel about teens life has been written by the author.

4. The governor will attend the ceremony.

PASSIVE: The ceremony will be attended by the governor.

PASSIVE: The meals are cooked by Nanda and her sister in the kitchen.

PASSIVE: Many trees are being planted by Yudi and his friends  at school.

7. The police arrested the robbers who escaped from the prison.

PASSIVE: The robbers who escaped from the prison were arrested by the police.

8. Alya keeps her private belongings in a special lock.

PASSIVE: Alya's private belongings are kept in a special lock.

9. Rafa will finish the job in several minutes.

PASSIVE: The job will be finished by Rafa in several minutes.
