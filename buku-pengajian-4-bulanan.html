x = 45 x 5/3

x = 75 halaman

2/5 x = 2/5 x (75) = 30 halaman

A]

= 45 : (1 - 2/5) × 2/5

= 45 : (5/5 - 2/5) × 2/5

= 45 : 3/5 × 2/5

= 45 × 5/3 × 2/5

= 30 halaman

B]

banyak halaman buku cerita itu seluruhnya

= 45 + 30
