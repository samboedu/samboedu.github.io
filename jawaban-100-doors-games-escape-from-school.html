5. A carpenter makes cabinets and desks. These items use the same types of doors and drawers. Each cabinet has 2 doors and 3 drawers, and each desk has 1 door and 5 drawers. The carpenter has 41 doors and 100 drawers available. How many of each item should he make to use his entire supply of doors and drawers?


Cabinet: 2 doors and 3 drawers

Desk: 1 door and 5 drawers

Available: 41 doors and 100 drawers

x= cabinet, y= desk

Door equation->

2x+y= 41

Drawer equation->

3x+5y= 100

y= 41-2x

3x+5(41-2x)= 100

3x+205-10x= 100

-7x= -105

x= 15

y= 41-2(15)

y= 41-30

y= 11

Answer:

15 cabinets and 11 desks

20. a A carpenter fitted 32 brown and white doors in a block of flats. He fitted 3 times as many brown doors as white doors. How many white doors did he fit in the block of flats?


Jawaban:

8 doors

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Total doors = 32

Brown doors : White doors = 3 : 1

White doors

= 1 / (3 + 1) × 32 doors

= 1/4 × 32 doors

= 32/4 doors

= 8 doors
