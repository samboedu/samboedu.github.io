6. kunci jawaban buku bahasa Inggris kelas 8 K13 halaman 129​


Degree of Comparison

2. Tasia is saying that the pencil case is smaller than the purse, but the purse is bigger than the pencil case.

3. According to Dwi, roses are prettier than other flowers.

4. Max wants to sit on the bench under the tree because it is shadier there.

5. According to Erman, the book ‘Malin Kundang’ is better than the film.

6. Yuni is saying that Edo is smarter than the other students.

Pembahasan

Comparative digunakan untuk membandingkan dua buah objek, yang salah satu objeknya memiliki sifat "lebih" dari objek lainnya.

Contoh: fast > faster (cepat > lebih cepat)

Superlative digunakan untuk menyatakan satu objek yang memiliki sifat “paling” jika di bandingan dengan objek lainnya.

Contoh: fast > the fastest (cepat > paling cepat)

Namun, ada beberapa kata sifat yang memiliki sifat ireguler. Untuk kata-kata tersebut maka kalian harus sedikit menghafalkan bentuk comparative dan superlativenya. Beberapa kata sifat itu adalah:

Well - better - bestBadly - worse - worstLittle - less - leastMuch - more - mostFar - further - furthest

Nah, untuk kata-kata dengan dua, tiga atau lebih syllables (suku kata), kita menggunakan more untuk membandingkan secara comparative dan menggunakan most untuk superlative.

Contohnya seperti kata-kata berikut:

Thoughtful (2 syllables) => Thought + ful menjadi more thoughtful/the most thoughtfulIntelligent (4 syllables) => In + tel + li + gent menjadi more intelligent dan the most intelligent

17. buku bahasa inggris kelas 7 K13 halaman 109


Buku Bahasa Inggris kelas VII halaman 109 adalah menceritakan tentang kualitas benda-benda dan ruangan yang kamu miliki di sekolah. Pada perintah soal, kamu diminta memilih 10 objek (benda dan ruangan) untuk didiskusikan dan kemudian menceritakan kualitasnya.

Pembahasan

This is our school.

1. It is large, clean and green. There are  many shady places. (for example)

2. Our class have [number] classes. All of them are very large.

3. This is our class. It is very spacious. The facilities are complete.

4. There are two blackboards [or whiteboards] in front of our class. They are new and clean.

5. There are [number] tables in our class. They are old and messy. There are a lot of writings on it.

6. There are [number] chairs in our class. They are small but comfortable.

7. Next to my class, they are the restrooms. They are always clean and smell good in the morning. But, in the afternoon, they are sometimes dirty and make us dizzy with the smell.

8. There is a schoolyard in front of our class. It is very large and tidy. We can play football and basketball at there.

9. There is a canteen in our school. It is small but clean and neat.

10. There is a parking lot near the canteen. It is long and neat. We can put our bicycles at there.

11. There is a teacher's office in front of the parking lot. It is large but messy. The teachers' tables are big, so the pathway is very narrow. Our teachers put the books on their tables, so it doesn't look neat and uncomfortable.
