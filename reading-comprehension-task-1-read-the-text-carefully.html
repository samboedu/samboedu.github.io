24. Task 1 : Read the text carefully. Your teacher will identify you as A or B. Students identified as A, read text 1 ; students as B, read text 2. Task 2 : After reading the text, in the chart below, identify the main ideas of the paragraphs, and then write the most important details in your own words. WEY BANTUIN GUA LAH. AYOLAH BANTULAH.


Read the text carefully and identify the main idea of the paragraphs to complete the chart below.JawabanPendahuluan

You have to work together with your partner to do this task. If you choose to read text 1, your partner has to read text 2. Make sure that you and your partner to identify the main ideas of the paragraphs. Both of you also need to write the most important details using your own words.

The first text is an announcement about concert cancellation. The second text is also an announcement but about the start of new term for McMaster Mini-Med School. To identify the main idea, you have to read the text carefully and find the information given by both announcements.

Pembahasan

Text 1.

- Main idea of paragraph 1: The Faith & D Entertainment Management announces the cancellation of the concert.

- Details:

The management has just been notified by Cjes Entertainment, the artist agency of JYJ.CJes Entertainment has decided to cancel JYJ World Tour Concert in Singapore.The concert is scheduled on 23 April 2011.The concert is held at Singapore Indoor Stadium.

- Main idea of paragraph 2: The Faith & D Entertainment have to accept the cancellation.

- Details:

The Faith & D Entertainment have submitted the final plans for stage, seating, and ticketing for the agency's approval.Those plans are in accordance with the rules set by the authorities in Singapore.CJes rejected the plan and decided to cancel the concert.Faith & D Entertainment respect CJes' decision to cancel the concert and can't change CJes mind.

- Main idea of paragraph 3: The Faith & D Entertainment apologize to JYJ fans.

- Details:

The Faith & D Entertainment understands that fans must be very disappointed.The management also apologize for the inconvenience they have caused.

Text 2.

- Main idea of paragraph 1: The McMaster Mini-Med school announces the commencement of the 2009 program.

- Details:

The school welcomes the 2009 students.The term lasts for seven weeks.The term begins Tuesday, March 3, 2009.

- Main idea of paragraph 2: Registration will occur on a first-come basis.

- Details:

They received a great response last year.It's advised to register as soon as possible.If the spots for students are full, the applicants who still want to join will be placed in the waiting list.The waiting list applicants will be notified when the spots become available.

- Main idea of paragraph 3: The things participants will get.

- Details:

Participants will get 6 things including a reserved spot for the Mini-Med school 2009, tote bag, clipboard and pen, blanket, and certificate as proof of completion of the program.

- Main idea of paragraph 4: Further details on how to register, fee, and list of speakers.

- Details:

Prospective applicants can check the link provided in the announcement to check registration fee and list of speakers.Applicants can register online by visiting the last link given in the announcement by the committee of the program.
