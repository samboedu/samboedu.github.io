2. Contoh kalimat spok dalam bahasa inggris, isi juga penjelasan spok nya


He went swimming with his friends yesterday.
( Indo = Ia pergi berenang dengan teman-temannya kemaren.)

Subjek → He (Ia)
Predikat (kata kerja atau apa yang sedang ia lakukan) → went swimming (pergi berenang)
Objek → with his friends (dengan teman2nya)
Keterangan → yeterday (kemaren)
Answer:
________
She goes to school everyday

She = Subject
Goes = predicate
School = object
Everyday = adverb
