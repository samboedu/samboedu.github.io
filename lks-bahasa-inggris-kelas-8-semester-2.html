6. jawab buku lks bahasa inggris kelas 8 hal 57 semester 2​


Narrative

            I have a bad experience which I still (1) regret up to now. It happened in the second semester of Grade Seven. I am really (2) into Science, especially Biology. Since I like it very much, I always receive very good (3) marks. The teacher is proud of me and often (4) praises me above the other students.

             Once I would have a Biology test. The day before the test, I had many assignments to do so I felt very (5) exhausted. As a result, I was reluctant to study for the test.

            I didn't want to receive a bad mark, so I (6) preferred to do a bad thing which I had never done before.  I cheated by writing the answers on a small piece of paper.

             On the D-day, I could do test easily because I just (7) peeped at the paper and then wrote the answers.  Unluckily, while I was copying the answers for the last question, my teacher was (8) standing behind me. She immediately took my paper and (9) disqualified me. I was very embarrassed and regretful.

           I understood that my teacher was disappointed too. My previous efforts on receiving best marks were (10) ruined. Everyone thought that I received good marks because I cheated. I hope you learn a lesson from my bad experience.

Pembahasan

Narrative (story) is any report of connected events, presented in a sequence of written or spoken words, and/or in a sequence of (moving) pictures.

The Structure of Narrative Text:

Orientation : introduces characters and settings or the scenes of the story (who, where, when and etc.) Complication : the main characters faces problems Resolution : the problem is solved, statement of moral values

Terjemahannya:

Narasi (cerita) adalah kisah atau cerita tentang peristiwa-peristiwa yang saling terhubung, disajikan dalam urutan kata-kata secara tertulis atau lisan, dan/atau dalam urutan gambar (bergerak).

Struktur Teks Naratif:

Orientasi: memperkenalkan karakter dan setting atau adegan cerita (siapa, di mana, kapan, dan lain-lain.)

Komplikasi: karakter utama menghadapi masalah -masalah

Penyelesaian: masalah terpecahkan/terselesaikan, pernyataan nilai-nilai moral

- shovel : sekop

Shovel is a tool commonly used to transport sand. This shovel tool is made from used drum plates such as osrok farming tools. Used this tool is relatively light to carry. This shovel is divided into three parts, namely the head, the middle, and the handle. In this part of the head forms a plate as a forming part. In the middle part is a vertical grip section consisting of garan made of wood. While on the upper handle / horizontal handle it forms a triangle. On the right and left side is made of a drum plate which is used to hold horizontally, there is also a sheet of wood. How to use this shovel is to put your left hand in the middle and right hand on the top handle. Then just swing to move. This tool is widely used by traditional sand miners and construction workers to make it easier for them to work on sand.
