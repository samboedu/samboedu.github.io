3. A carpenter makes cabinets and desks. These items use the same types of doors and drawers. Each cabinet has 2 doors and 3 drawers, and each desk has 1 door and 5 drawers. The carpenter has 41 doors and 100 drawers available. How many of each item should he make to use his entire supply of doors and drawers?


Cabinet: 2 doors and 3 drawers

Desk: 1 door and 5 drawers

Available: 41 doors and 100 drawers

x= cabinet, y= desk

Door equation->

2x+y= 41

Drawer equation->

3x+5y= 100

y= 41-2x

3x+5(41-2x)= 100

3x+205-10x= 100

-7x= -105

x= 15

y= 41-2(15)

y= 41-30

y= 11

Answer:

15 cabinets and 11 desks

PEMBAHASANActive Voice adalah Kalimat aktif, subject melakukan tindakan atau aksi dalam kalimatnyaPassive Voice adalah Kalimat Pasif, Subject yg dikenai tindakan atau aksi oleh Object

1. Present Tense

Active Voice:

(+) s + Verb (-s/es) + O

siska opens the doors

Passive Voice:

(+) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + V3 + By

The doors are opened by siskaPenjelasan:The doors => pintu-pintuMaka tobe => are

Kecuali

The door => pintu Maka Tobe => is

2. Present Continuous

Active Voice:

(+) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + V-ing + O

ramdan is opening the doors

Passive Voice:

(+) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + V3 + B

The doors are being opened by ramdanPenjelasan:The doors => pintu-pintuMaka tobe => are

Kecuali

The door => pintu Maka Tobe => is

3. Present Perfect

Active Voice:

(+) S + has/have + V3 + O

Afghan has opened the door

Passive Voice:

(+) S + has/have + been + V3 + By

The door has been opened by AfghanPenjelasan:The door => pintuMaka => has

Kecuali

The doors => pintu-pintu Maka => have

4. Past Tense

Active Voice:

(+) S + Verb 2 + O

Anwar opened the doors

Passive Voice:

(+) S + Was/Were + V3 + By

The doors were opened by AnwarPenjelasan:The doors => pintu-pintuMaka tobe => were

Kecuali

The door => pintu Maka Tobe => was

5. Past Continuous

Active Voice:

(+) S + To be (was/were) + V-ing + O

wirda was opening the doors

Passive Voice:

(+) S + Tobe (was/were) + being + V3 + By

=> The doors were being opened by wirda

Penjelasan:The doors => pintu-pintuMaka tobe => were

Kecuali

The door => pintu Maka Tobe => was

6. Future Tense

Active Voice:

(+) S + will + V1 + O

Ajeng will open the door

Passive Voice:

(+) S + will + be + V3 + by

The door will be opened by ajeng

24. a A carpenter fitted 32 brown and white doors in a block of flats. He fitted 3 times as many brown doors as white doors. How many white doors did he fit in the block of flats?


Jawaban:

8 doors

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Total doors = 32

Brown doors : White doors = 3 : 1

White doors

= 1 / (3 + 1) × 32 doors

= 1/4 × 32 doors

= 32/4 doors

= 8 doors

30. 7. Pesta olahraga ASEAN untuk para atlet difabel disebut...a. SEA GAMESb. ASEAN PARA GAMESC. ASIAN GAMESd. ASIAN PARA GAMESplis jawab harus sekarang :(​


Jawaban:

Opsi D.

Penjelasan:

Asian Para Games merupakan suatu pesta olahraga se-Asia Tenggara untuk para atlet penyandang disabilitas. Jenis olahraganya pun berbeda dengan turnamen olahraga pada umumnya.

Pesta Olahraga Difabel Asia Tenggara (bahasa Inggris: ASEAN Para Games) adalah ajang olahraga dua tahunan yang diadakan setelah Pesta Olahraga Asia Tenggara (SEA Games) untuk atlet-atlet yang mengalami cacat fisik (difabel). ASEAN ParaGames diikuti oleh 11 negara yang terletak di Asia Tenggara. Ajang ini mengikuti konsep dan merupakan ajang persiapan Paralimpiade. ASEAN ParaGames di bawah pengawasan ASEAN Para Sports Federation (APSF). Tuan rumah penyelenggaraan ajang ini sama dengan negara penyelenggara SEA Games.
