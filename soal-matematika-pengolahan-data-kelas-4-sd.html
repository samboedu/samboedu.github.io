Jawaban terlampir

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

A. 1 1/12 + 2 12/13 =?

1 1/12 + 2 12/13 = (1 + 2) = 3

1/12 + 12/13 = (1×13), (12×12), (12×13) = (13+144)/156 =

157/156 + 3 = 3 157/156

B. 3 5/6 + 5 =?

3 5/6 + 5 = (3 + 5) = 8

8 + 5/6 = 8 5/6

C. 4 7/8 + 4 2/8 =?

4 7/8 + 4 2/8 = (4 + 4) = 8

7/8 + 2/8 = (7×8), (2×8) = (56+16)/8 = 72/8 + 8 = 8 72/8

D. 8 + 5 1/10 =?

8 + 5 1/10 = (8 + 5) = 13

13 + 1/10 = 13 1/10

E. 7 10/11 + 3 4/5 =?

7 10/11 + 3 4/5 = (7 + 5) = 12

10/11 + 4/5 = (10×5), (4×11), (11×5) = (50 + 44)/55 =

94/55 + 12 = 12 94/55

F. 3 - 1 1/9 =?

3 - 1 1/9 = (3 - 1) = 2

2 + 1/9 = 2 1/9

G. 4 9/10 - 2 2/7 =?

4 9/10 - 2 2/7 = (4 - 2) = 2

9/10 - 2/7 = (9 - 7), (10 - 2), (10 - 7) = (2 + 8)/3 =

10/3 + 2 = 2 10/3

H. 8 8/11 - 4 =?

8 8/11 - 4 = (8 - 4) = 4 + 8/11 = 4 8/11

I. 10 2/11 - 4 3/13 =?

10 2/11 - 4 3/13 = (10 - 4) = 6

2/11 - 3/13 = (13 - 2), (11 - 3), (13 - 11) = (11 + 8)/2 =

19/2 + 6 = 6 19/2

J. 12 - 5 4/19 =?

12 - 5 4/19 = (12 - 5) = 7 + 4/19 = 7 4/19
