1. Neni : Would you like to accompany me to the shop?

Rina : ... I want to buy some snack too.

Neni : OK, then. Let’s go!

A. I’m not B. Sorry C. Certainly D. I have doubt

2. Anang : Did you do the test very well, Andi?

Andi : ..... I don’t have a hope of getting even 50.

Anang : But are you sure?

Andi : Absolutely. Not a hope!

Anang : Don’t worry too much. Next time better.

A. Sorry B. I’m sure C. Certainly D. I’m not so sure

3. Marko : I have good news for you. Your article on the tourism in Indramayu won the national competition.

Jaka : ....? It’s what I want to hear. Where can I find the information about it?

Marko : I read the announcement in front of the teacher’s office.

Jaka : I want to see it. Thanks for the information.

A. Well B. Really C. Oh, I see D. Sure

4. Kesya : Hello, Resty. Look at my new purse. I made it myself.

Resty : Hello, Kesya. Hey, that’s nice. Isn’t that your old purse? The one that we bought one year ago?

Kesya : Yup, that’s right.

Resty : How did you do that?

Kesya : Sorry ...

Resty : How did you do that, Kesya?

Kesya : Oh. Well, first, I just added some colourful buttons.Then, four–five glittery beads. Finally,I gave my magic finishing touch, hahaha.

A. What did you say? C. What do you want?

B. What did you do D. What are you doing?

5. Dona : Hey, Gun. What are you doing?

Gun : I’m making a windmill from paper. Ah, now it is finished.

Dona : Wow,...... ! How did you do that?

Gun : I made it from a square piece of paper by cutting inwards from each corner halfway to the centre point. Then, I folded over each point with a dot on it and tape it to the centre.

A. It’s easy B. It’s terrific C. It’s difficult D. It’s dangerous

To : Ivone

Dear Ivone,

By this letter I am proud to tell you about some changes in my life that I have done so far. I have just finished my computer class in Pioneer College. I have taken the course since July last year, and I have learnt about office program for ten months.

Wish me luck,

Fani

Kategori : bahasa inggris - short functional text
Kelas; SMP IX


NOTICE
Private property
KEEP OUT

1. What does the notice above mean?
A. People have to keep their properties.
B. People should stay away from the area.
C. Only certain people allowed to enter the room.
D. Not all people have to keep out their private properties.
answer : C.

Santi, our plan to visit our friends who suffer from Aceh earthquake is cancelled. the weather is not good to fly. i will let you know later.
(Gumanti)

2. How do Santi and Gumanti plan to visit their friends?
A. By bus.
B. By ship.
C. By train.
D. by plane.
answer : D.

3. The short message is written to inform about …
A. when to go to Aceh
B. the weather in Aceh
C. Santi condition in Aceh
D. the cancellation of the plan
answer: D.

Mr. and Mrs. Samuel Hansen

Request the company of
Mr. and Mrs. Ben Johnson Brown

On the occasion of their
25th Wedding Anniversary
On Saturday 28 December at 7 pm
Gedongkuning Complex Block 3D No. 902
Bantul Yogyakarta

R.S.V.P. Gumanti (08123456789)

4. Who will celebrate the wedding anniversary?
A. Gumanti.
B. Mr. and Mrs. Brown.
C. Mr. and Mrs. Hansen.
D. The company of Mr. and Mrs. Brown.
answer : C.

5. The text is written in order to …
A. announce the wedding anniversary party.
B. inform the marriage of Mr. and Mrs. Hansen.
C. invite someone to the wedding anniversary party.
D. congratulate the marriage of Mr. and Mrs. Brown.

CLEAN UP
Antiseptic Soap
Shake well before use
10 ml of soap for every 3 liters of water.
Take a bath with the mixed water.
Rinse afterwards.
Caution: For external use only.
If swallowed call a doctor.

6. What is the suitable suggestion before using it?
A. Clean up.
B. Swallow it.
C. Shake well.
D. Take a bath.
answer : C.

7. The text above is written in order to …
A. persuade people to buy the product.
B. give detailed information of the product.
C. explain how to use the product.
D. give warning of the product.
answer: B.

No Littering
The garbage can cause flood
in rainy season

8. What does the text mean?
A. People are suggested to put the rubbish in the area.
B. People are banned to throw the rubbish in the area.
C. Only in the rainy season, people are not allowed to put the rubbish there.
D. People are permitted to put the garbage there, especially in the dry season.
answer: B.

Look for a used car (Daihatsu, Suzuki or Toyota). +/- 10 years old. not more than Rp.35.000.000m
Call Gumanti 08123456789.

9. What is Gumanti looking for?
A. Daihatsu.
B. Suzuki.
C. Toyota.
D. A used car.
answer : D.

A. the writer wants a new car.
B. Gumanti is the owner of the car being offered.
C. the maximum price of the car is Rp.35.000.000.
D. you may call 08123456789 to get information of the car.
