Jawaban:

1.

Present: You should be greeting and making friend with someone beside you

2.

Present: You should be throwing garbages in a trash can

Future: You should throw garbages in a trash can

3.

Present: You should be going to doctor

Future: You should go to doctor

Past: You should have gone to doctor

Penjelasan:

Present continuous tense adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk memberitahu keadaan yang sedang berlangsung

Future tense adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk memberitahu keadaan yang akan terjadi di masa mendatang

Past tense adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk memberitahu keadaan yang telah terjadi di masa lalu

Jawaban:

congratulation

noun

1. The act of expressing joy or acknowledgment, as for the achievement or good fortune of another.

2. often congratulations An expression of such joy or acknowledgment: sent him my congratulations on winning the award.

interjection

congratulations

Used to express such joy or acknowledgment: I heard you got a promotion. Congratulations!

Origin

From Middle French, con- +‎ gratulation, cognate with Latin congratulor (congrātulor, “I congratulate”).

29. sastra Inggris halaman 91 kelas 10​


Jawaban

Thatcher was born in Grantham, Lincolnshire, on October 13 1925. Her father was Alfred Roberts and her mother was Beatrice Ethel. When she was a child, they lived in Grantham, where her father ran two grocery shops. Her parents raised her and her older sister, Muriel, in the flat above the grocery shop on North Parade near the railway line. Her father was an active member of Liberal party. He brought up his daughters strictly. He was appointed the Mayor of Grantham in 1945-1946. When the Labour party won (teks terpotong) he lost his position as alderman.

Penjelasan

Teks di atas merupakan biography (biografi) dari seseorang bernama Thatcher.

Ciri khas dari teks biografi adalah menggunakan simple past tense dikarenakan menceritakan sesuatu yang terjadi di masa lampau.

Simple past tense menggunakan kata kerja bentuk kedua (Verb 2)

Maka kita bisa melengkapi teks tersebut menggunakan kata kerja bentuk kedua (V2) sebagai berikut:

Was (subjek singular, Thatcher)Was (subjek singular, her father)Was (subjek singular, her mother)Was (subjek singular, she)Live - Lived - Lived (regular verb)Run - Ran - Run (irregular verb)Raise - Raised - Raised (regular verb)Was (subjek singular, her father)Bring up - Brought up - Brought up (irregular verb)Was (subjek singular, he)Win - Won - Won (irregular verb)Lose - Lost - Lost (irregular verb)

Catatan!

To be past = Was/were (was untuk subjek singular, were untuk subjek plural)Regular Verb = Kata kerja lampau berakhiran -edIrregular Verb = Kata kerja lampau tidak berakhiran -ed
