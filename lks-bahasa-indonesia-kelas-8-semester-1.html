Jawaban:

Joan : Nada, do you think English is difficult?

Nada : I don't think so, there is no difficult lesson.

Joan : Excuse me, I don't quite follow you.

Nada : Haha, that's my words!

Joan : In my opinion, it is difficult because I am hard to do every task that is given by our teacher

Nada : It is because you are lack vocabulary. By the way, what do you think about the English schedule in our class?

Joan : It's good. There is no problem with that.

Nada : I don't think so, I believe if we do English is in the morning, it will be easier to do the lesson. I am hard to follow that schedule

Joan : But I think if we learn seriously there is no hard in it. it depends on us, right?

Nada : Yes, I think if we learn seriously, there is no difficult lesson, so it depends on us.

Joan : Yeah, I think so too.

(Juga, Nada itu seorang munafik karena mengatakan bahwa Joan "lack Vocabulary" ketika dia sendiri memiliki kosakata yang buruk)
