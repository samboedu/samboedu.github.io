4. possessive adjective adalah


Possessive Adjective: kata ganti yang menunjukkan/menyatakan kepunyaan. Di dalam possessive adjective bendanya harus disebutkan/ditulis.

•Orang Pertama
Tunggal: My
Jamak: Our
•Orang Kedua
Tunggal: Your
Jamak: Your
•Orang Ketiga
Tunggal: His, Her, Its (tdk pakai tanda petik)
Jamak: Their

Contoh:
-This is MY HOUSE
-That is YOUR BOOK
-Is that YOUR RULER?

5. write the examples about possessive adjective/possessive prouncecontoh nya


Possessive Adjective merupakan kata ganti yang berhubungan dengan menghubungkan akan kepemilikan kepada seseorang atau sesuatu.

Examples :
Your dress is so beautiful. (your sebagai possessive Adjective).
I like her new hair. (her sebagai possessive Adjective)

Possessive Pronoun artinya sama dengan Possessive Adjective yaitu sebagai kata ganti berhubungan dengan kepemilikan seseorang atau sesuatu. Biasanya untuk kata possessive pronoun ditaruh akhir kalimat.
possessive pronouns are mine, yours, his, hers, its, ours, yours, and theirs. 

Examples:
The book on the table is mine. (mine sebagai possessive pronoun)
The hat is yours now. (yours is possessive pronoun)

6. Sebutkan 5 contoh kalimat possessive adjective dan possessive pronouns


Untuk membuat kalimat possessive adjective dan possessive pronouns kita harus memperhatikan kalimat dengan menggunakan subjective pronoun terlebih dahulu. Contoh kalimat possessive adjective dan possessive pronouns adalah

1. I have a new bag.

Possessive adjective: My bag is blue.

Possessive pronoun: This blue bag is mine.

2. You have two cat.

Possessive adjective: Your cats are very cute.

Possessive pronoun: Those cute cats are yours.

3. He always waters the plants everyday.

Possessive adjective: His plants are very fertile.

Possessive pronoun: These plants are his.

4. We play kites together.

Possessive adjective: Our kites fly so high.

Possessive pronoun: Ours have many colours and beautiful.

5. They have funny rabbits.

Possessive adjective: Their rabbits play in the backyard.

Possessive pronoun:  Theirs eat a lot of carrots.
