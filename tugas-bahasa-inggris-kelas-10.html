9. tugas bahasa inggris halaman 23-24 kelas 10 edisi revisi 2016​


All of those people congratulate Alif because Alif is appointed as the director of a national company where he works.They use expressions of congratulations to congratulate Alif.Alif responds to their congratulationg expressions with the expressions of gratitude, such as "Thank you", "Thank you very much".The social purpose of congratulating people is generally to appreciate someone's achievement.We congratulate people when they got or achieve something in their life.The expressions commonly used to congratulate people are:Congratulations!I'm happy for you!You've done a great job!Well done!

Teks 1 secara singkat menceritakan pencapaian Alif yang ditunjuk sebagai direktur perusahaan tempat di mana dia bekerja. Banyak dari rekan-rekan kerjanya memberi ucapan selamat atas pencapaianya tersebut.

Pembahasan

Expressions of congratulation adalah ungkapan-ungkapan pemberian selamat kepada seseorang atas suatu hal. Pada dialog, ungkapan pemberian selamat dilakukan oleh rekan-rekan kerja Alif karena Alif telah ditunjuk sebagai direktur perusahaan nasional tempat di mana dia bekerja.

Berikut terjemahan soal dan penjelasan detail jawaban di atas.

1. Mengapa semua orang tersebut memberi selamat kepada Alif?

Hal ini karena Alif akhirnya ditunjuk sebagai direktur perusahaan nasional tempat di mana dia bekerja. Perhatikan paragraf pertama kalimat pertama pada teks 1!

2. Ekspresi apa yang mereka gunakan untuk memberi selamat kepada Alif?

Mereka menggunakan ekspresi ucapan selamat (expressions of congratulations). Ekspresi ini diucapkan ketika seseorang mendapatkan atau meraih sesuatu.

3. Bagaimana Alif menanggapi ucapan selamat mereka?

Ali menanggapi dengan ungkapan terima kasih, seperti "Thank you", "Thank you very much". Tampak pada dialog beberapa ungkapan terimakasih Alif kepada rekan-rekan kerjanya.

4. Apa tujuan sosial memberi selamat kepada orang lain?

Memberi selamat kepada orang lain bertujuan untuk menghargai orang lain atas pencapaian yang didapatkan atau diraih dalam hidupnya. Hal ini dapat menambah keakraban dalam hubungan sosial.

5. Kapan kita memberi selamat kepada orang lain?

Ketika orang lain mendapatkan atau meraih sesuatu dalam kehidupannya atau pekerjaannya. Hal ini sangat umum dilakukan. Dalam hal ini, Alif meraih sesuatu yaitu ditunjuk sebagai direktur.

6. Apa ekspresi yang umumnya digunakan untuk memberi selamat kepada orang lain?

Ekspresi itu dapat berupa:

Congratulations! (Selamat!)I'm happy for you. (Aku ikut senang)You've done a great job! (Kamu telah melakukan pekerjaan yang hebat!)Well done! (Bagus, keren!)

18. jawaban tugas bahasa inggris kelas 10 halaman 127​


GRAMMAR REVIEW
Bacalah kalimat tidak lengkap di bawah ini. Lengkapilah dengan menggunakan to be "am/are/is" atau "was/were".

Pembahasan :
1). PRESENT TENSE (masa sekarang)
* To be (am) digunakan apabila subject nya "I".
* To be (are) digunakan apabila subject nya you/they/we/plural subject.
* To be (is) digunakan apabila subject nya she/he/it/kata ganti orang ketiga tunggal.

2). PAST TENSE (masa lampau)
* To be (were) digunakan apabila subject nya you/they/we/plural subject.
* To be (was) digunakan apabila subject nya I/she/he/it/kata ganti orang ketiga tunggal.


Jadi, jawabannya adalah :
1). In 1945, they (WERE) 17, so they (ARE) 85 now.

2). Today the weather (IS) Cold, but Last monday it (WAS) terribly hot.

3). I (AM) very thirsty. Can somebody give me a glass of water, please?

4). The defiant freedom frighters (WERE) very brave. They fought till death to defend the city.

5). I (AM) happy with Surabaya now. Years ago, It (WAS) very dirty and messy.

6). Don't buy those weapons. They (ARE) dangerous and illegal.

7). Hey, I like your new hat. It fits you well, and when you wear it, it reminds me of the 1945 freedom fighters. (IS) it expensive?

8). This time seventy years ago my grandfather (WAS) in Surabaya Joining the militia to fight against the British Army.

9). Where (ARE) the veterans? I don't know. They (WERE) in the lobby of the hotel five minutes ago.

10). The generals and the veterans (ARE) happy and optimistic about us now. They know we (ARE) always busy with good activities and never think of using drugs. When we met them last year, they (WERE) pessimistic.

GRAMMAR REVIEW  (Simple Present dan Simple Past)

were, are is,was am were am, was are is was are, were are, are, were

PEMBAHASAN:

Simple Present Tense  

Simple present tense digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta /kebenaran umum (general truth), kebiasaan (habitual action), atau kejadian yang terjadi pada saat ini (present). Simple present tense paling sering digunakan dalam bahasa Inggris karena digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan sehari-hari.  

Formula:  

Verbal sentences:  

(+) Subject + Verb1 s/es  

(-) Subject + do/does + not + Verb1  

(?) Do/does + Subject + Verb1?  

Nominal sentence:  

(+) Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Object/Complement  

(-) Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Object/Complement  

(?) To be (is/am/are) +Subject + Object/Complement?  

Contoh:  

1. (+) Susi brings her dictionary  

  (-) Susi does not bring her dictionary  

  (?) Does Susi bring her dictionary?

2. (+) My mom is very diligent

   (-) My mom is not very diligent

   (?) Is My mom very diligent?

3. (+) My father is in the office

   (-) My father is not in the office

   (?) Is My father in the office?

4. (+) Ammar and Amira are very smart

   (-) Ammar and Amira are not very smart

   (?) Are Ammar and Amira very smart?

5. (+) Egra and Yudi are always friendly

     (-) Egra and Yudi are not always friendly

     (?) Are Egra and Yudi always friendly?

6. (+) Susi is very diligent

    (-) Susi is not very diligent

    (?) Is Susi very diligent?

Cara menambahkan akhiran s atau es pada kata kerja:

Akhiran -es ditambahkan pada kata kerja (verb) yang berhuruf akhir -ch (catches), -s (kisses), -sh (brushes), -x (fixes), -z (whizzes) Akhiran -es juga ditambahkan pada kata kerja (veb) yang berhuruf akhir -y yang didahului oleh huruf mati dan akan berubah menjadi -i jika mendapat tambahan akhiran -es tersebut. contoh: study=studies, carry=carries, try=tries. Sedangkan bila -y didahului oleh huruf hidup, maka kerja itu hanya akan mendapat akhiran -s. contoh :pay-pays, say=says Akhiran -es juga ditambahkan khusus bagi kata kerja do dan go. contoh: do=does, go=goes

Keterangan Waktu Simple Pesent Tense:

always = selalu every = tiap often = sering usually = biasanya seldom = jarang rarely = jarang- jarang generally = pada umumnya frequently = seringkali sometimes = kadang-kadang ever = pernah never = tak pernah

Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Time expression: yesterday, last two days, last year, this morning, a week ago, ...etc.

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2

(-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  

(?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C

(-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  

(?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?

Contoh :

1. (+) Susi took a bath yesterday

  (-) Susi did not take a bath yesterday

  (?) Did Susi take a bath yesterday?

2. (+) Susi was an artist

    (-) Susi was not an artist

    (?) Was Susi an artist?

3. (+) Susi brought her dictionary  

    (-) Susi did not bring her dictionary  

    (?) Did Susi bring her dictionary ?

4. (+) My father drank a cup of coffee this morning

    (-) My father did not drink a cup of coffee this morning

    (?) Did My father drink a cup of coffee this morning?

5.(+) My mom was very diligent

   (-) My mom was not very diligent

   (?) Was My mom very diligent?

6. (+) My father was in the office  yesterday afternoon  

     (-) My father was not in the office  yesterday afternoon  

     (?) Was My father in the office  yesterday afternoon?

Recount Text

Recount Text adalah salah satu jenis text dalam bahasa Inggris yang menceritakan kembali tentang kejadian-kejadian atau pengalaman-pengalaman di masa lampau. Tujuan dari Recount Text adalah untuk memberikan informasi atau untuk menghibur pembaca sehingga tidak terdapat konflik.  

Text organization:

Orientation : berisi tentang pengenalan mengenai latar belakang cerita, kapan , dimana dan orang-orang yang trerlibat di dalam cerita Event :merupakan rangkaian kejadian di dalam cerita yang di ceritakan secara kronologis Reorientation : kesimpulan yang merupakan komentar penulis mengenai kejadian.

Unsur Kebahasaan:

Menggunakan noun dan pronoun, contoh: Afgen, I, he, we, they Menggunakan action verbs , contoh: went, played, spent Menggunakan time conjunction, contoh: when, before, after, dan finally Menggunakan past tense

Perhatiakan bentuk simple past tense pada teks berikut!

Afgan has always been my favourite singer. I had always been thinking of how I would feel when I met him. Then I was suddenly hit by lightning when I found out Afgan was coming to town for a concert in a local auditorium. A day before the concert, there would be a meet-and-greet event at a local radio station. Feeling excited, I packed all my Afgan’s CDs to get his signature at the event.

On that bright and sunny Saturday morning, the radio station was full of Afganism (that’s how Afgan’s fans are called). They sat on the chairs prepared inside the radio station’s lobby. Some stood in rows in the front yard of the radio station. A spot inside a lobby was prepared with a mini stage for Afgan’s singing performance and a table for Afgan to sign Afganism’s memorabilia. Finally, after about 40 or 50 minutes wait, Afgan showed up from inside the radio station. He smiled and waved to all Afganism who had been boring. How are you all?”. The crowd went crazy. The shouts sounded like a mix of “Fine, thank you” and screams of Afgan’s name.
