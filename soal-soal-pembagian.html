PART C

Bentuk tag question dari kalimat di atas adalah..

Answer :

2). They are good friends, aren't they?

3). They watch television every night, don't they?

4). You enjoy your English class, don't you?

5). The mail is delivered at ten o"clock, isn't it?

Kata "it" sebagai kata ganti dari "the mail".

6). You spend a lot of time with them, don't you?

7). She has to work very hard, hasn't she?

8). He is too old to play football, isn't he?

9). You have piano lesson twice a week, don't you?

10). The plane arrives at noon, doesn't it?

Kata "it" sebagai kata ganti dari "the plane".

11). They visit you every Sunday, don't they?

12). You get up early every morning, don't you?

13). He sits in the front row, doesn't he?

14). She works in the front row, doesn't she?

15). He is a good typist, isn't he?

Penjelasan :

Untuk kalimat yang bentuk verbal seperti pada nomor 3, 4, 6, 9, 10 sampai 14 maka tag question nya menggunakan auxiliary verb. Karena tenses yang digunakan pada kalimat di atas adalah bentuk present tense, maka auxiliary verb nya yaitu DO/DOES.

Gunakan DO jika subject nya I/you/they/we/plural subject.Gunakan DOES jika subject nya she/he/it/etc.

PART D

Bentuk kalimat dan tag question past tense dari kalimat di atas adalah..

2). They were good friends, weren't they?

3). They watched television, didn't they?

4). You enjoy your English class, didn't you?

5). The mail was delivered at ten o'clock, wasn't it?

6). You spent a lot of time with them, didn't you?

7). She had to work very hard, hadn't she?

8). He was too old to play football, wasn't he?

9). You had piano lesson twice a week, didn't you?

10). The plane arrived at noon, didn't it?

11). They visited you, didn't they?

12). You got up early, didn't you?

13). He sat in the front row, didn't he?

14). She worked in the front row, didn't she?

15). He was a good typist, wasn't he?

Penjelasan

Untuk bagian D karena harus di ubah ke dalam bentuk past tense dulu, maka rumus yang digunakan yaitu :

Past nominal

S + To be (was/were) + complement

Gunakan WAS jika subject nya I/she/he/it.Gunakan WERE jika subject nya you/they/we.

Past Verbal

S + Verb-2 + O

Karena bentuk verbal, maka di tag questionnya menggunakan DID. Auxiliary verb DID digunakan untuk semua subject.

Catatan !!!

Jika kalimat positif, maka tag questionnya negatif.Jika kalimat nya negatif, maka tag questionnya positif.

JIKA JUMLAH SOAL KU ADA 30.15 SOAL PG(SOAL BAGIAN PERTAMA), 10 SOAL (SOAL BAGIAN KEDUA),5 SOAL BAGIAN AKHIR/KETIGA,KET:JUMLAH SOAL BAGIAN PERTAMA NILAI/POIN NYA HARUS SAMA.DAN BAGIAN KETIGA POIN NYA HARUS LEBIH BESAR DITANYA:TOLONG DONG YG BISA, BANTUIN, TERSERAH AJA KASIH POIN NYA BRAPA, YG PENTING ADIL ​


Jawab:

Soal PG diberi nilai 2

Soal bagian kedua diberi nilai 4

Soal bagian ketiga diberi nilai 6

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

soal pg bernilai 2, sehingga 2 x 15 = 30

soal bagian kedua bernilai 4, sehingga 4 x 10 = 40

soal bagian ketiga bernilai 6, sehingga 6 x 5 = 30

jadi 30 + 40 + 30 = 100
