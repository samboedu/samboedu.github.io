11. Tolong dijawab buat latihan USBN


k1: 70km / jam
k2: 50km / jam
jarak:150km
===============================
w = j / k1 + k2
=150 / 70 + 50 =150 / 120
=15 / 12 = 1 3/12 = 1 1/4
= 1.15 menit
waktu berpapasan = 9.45 + 1.15
= 10.60 = pk 11.00
jadikan jawaban terbaik
pertama rumus waktu papasan
j1 + j2 = j kota (j = jarak)
dan rumus jarak
j = v × t ( v = kecepatan,t = waktu)
ket.
pak randi = j1
pak rahman = j2

jadi
j (pak randi) + j (pak rahman)= j kota
v1 × t1 + v2 × t2 = 150km
(70km/jam × t) + (50km/jam × t) = 150km
120km/jam × t = 150km
t = (150km : 120km)jam
t = 1,25 jam ( ingat 1jam = 60 menit)
t = 1 jam 15 menit

jadi, mereka berpapasan 1 jam 15 menit setelah jam 09.45

= 09.45 + 1 jam 15 menit
= jam 11.00
