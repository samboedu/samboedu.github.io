Ep = m * g * h ,  cari massanya (m) = ρ air * Volume air = 1000*100 =100.000 kg
Ep = 1*10^5 * 10 * 12 = 12*10^6 Joule = 12 Mj

Daya = Energi / waktu
= 12 Mj / 1 s = 12 Mwatt
