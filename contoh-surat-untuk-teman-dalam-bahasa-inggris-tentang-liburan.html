6. contoh surat undangan berlibur dalam bahasa inggris


Last holiday, I visited my brother in Bandung. My brother stayed in her his house alone. I always visited him once a month. His home was in Lembang which was not so far from Bandung city. When I arrived there, I invited him to visit the famous places in Bandung city. My brother recommended so many interesting places, that I made a list where I had to visit.

The first place I visited was Curug Dago. It was one of the waterfalls in Bandung. My brother and I rode motorcycle to go to there. When I arrived there, I was so amazed of the wonderful view. The waterfall was not so high, it was only 10 meters. but it looked very beautiful. there were few people know about this waterfall, so the condition around the waterfall seemed natural.After visiting Curug Dago, I chose Trans Studio Bandung as the second destination. I was surprised when I knew the entrance price. It was 250.000 rupiah. I spent a lot of money just for visiting this place. But, the English proverb “The price doesn’t lie” was right. I rode many interesting and fun game there. There were about twenty games in Trans Studio. I also went to the mall to buy some souvenirs for my mother.Then, I went to the third place. The place was Ciater thermal baths. My brother and I took a bath there. I was so relax when I felt the hot water in the tube. After taking a bath, I tried to find some meals for dinner. I found a good restaurant around Ciater and tried some delicious food there. Then, I went to a cottage for sleeping.

On the next day, I remembered my mother’s order. She got me to buy clothes from Bandung. So, my brother told me about a good market to buy good clothes. He recommended Pasar Baru as my destination. So, I went to this market. There were so many people who sold many variants of clothes. I tried to bargain with the seller for a good price. Finally, I found the cheap price and bought so many clothes for my mother.

After buying the clothes, I went to my brother’s house. I rested and had a lunch there. Then, I packed my luggage and said goodbye to my brother. I promised to visit him next month.

8. Buatkan surat tentang liburan semester dalam bahasa inggris untuk teman sekelassss


My family and I went to my grandmother’s housein Yogyakarta last month. It was my first trip to this city. We went there two days after my sister’s gradution ceremony in Semarang. We arrived at Yogyakarta at night. We spent a week staying in my grandmother’s house which is 5 minutes away by foot to Malioboro street.

In the first morning, we were still too tired after a long trip from Semarang to Yogyakarta. So we decided to stay at home to recharge our energy. I walk around the neighborhood with my sister just to experience how it is like to be in Yogyakarta. There were too many house, I think, which made the space between a house and the other was so small, even the road was also small that only bicycle and motorcycle can go through.

On the second day, all of us went to Malioboro street. We saw so many merchant with various of product which they claim to be a traditional product of Yogyakarta. I bought some wooden figurine and T-shirt with the word “Yogyakarta” printed on it, while my sister bought some leather handbag. My mom and dad were busy choosing some merchandise to be brought home when we go back.

On the third day, we went to Taman Sari and Keraton Ngayogyakarta Hadiningrat to see some historical building in Yogyakarta. We took a lot of picture there. We also took some picture of the building so we can check it again at home. We found some place providing Yogya traditional food around the building and we jumped in right away.

We spent the rest of our week in Yogyakarta by visiting some Shopping Malls such as Jogja City Mall, Malioboro Mall, Hartono Mall and Ambarrukmo Plaza. We realized that Yogyakarta turned out to be very warm during the day, that was the reason why we decided to spend more time in air conditioned building like this.

10. Contoh pengalaman Liburan Bersama Teman teman pake bahasa inggris


Holidays With Friends
On 16th of August 2014 my friends and I decided to take a vacation to the Kiluan bay, South Lampung. This vacation was planned to celebrate a farewell party for our friends who were going to continue their study in Java. Well maybe this was our last moment to have vacation together. Because we believed that it would be difficult to gather again.One day earlier, Friday August 15, we stayed at our friend’s home first. On the next day, we went there by motorcycle. The trip we travelled was quite difficult. So we spent 4 hours to arrive there. Having arrived there, we took a rest and prayed dhuhur. Afterwards, we crossed the Kiluan bay using a small boat to the Kiluan Island. It was an incredibly beautiful view. natural sea and clear sea water surface were the main attraction for me.

It only took 20 minutes to arrive the Kiluan Island. Once there, we rented a cottage and set up a tent for our night there. After Ashar prayer, we sat under the trees on the beach, enjoying our afternoon with the buzzing of the waves. We spent that night with joking and singing, of course, with baking fish for our dinner. It’s been 2 am we mingled with the beach. Finally, each one of us were crashed too, defeated by great drowsiness. Just the three of us who still survived until dawn came.

In the morning, a small boat picked up us to take us to see dolphins, Yup, There were a lot of dolphins, and this was the main attraction for tourists visiting here. However, I decided to go around the Kiluan Island alone. I was more interested in interacting with nature with my silence. It’s beautiful creation of my Lord, I thought. That day passed very quickly, we had to go home before night.

It’s a very short vacation, but I hope one day we can meet again and vacation together in a more beautiful place.

20. contoh surat tentang liburan dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya (surat nya yang agak panjang)


Dear Regina,

How are you? I hope you’re fine. I’m writing this letter to tell you that I really miss you. Do you miss me too? It’s too bad that we couldn’t spend the last holiday together. I spent my holiday in Malang. I visited some places in the city, but among those places, Mount Bromo left the greatest impression on me.

Let me tell you how my tour in Mount Bromo went. I spent about 3 hours to get to Mount Bromo from Malang by public transportations. I arrived at the location in the afternoon. Since I wanted to watch the sunrise from the top of Penanjakan Hill which was one of the best sunrise spot in Mount Bromo, I decided to take a rest at a guest house and would go to the hill at the dawn. I woke up at 3 in the morning and as soon as I was ready, I rent a jeep to get to Penanjakan Hill. When arriving at the hill, many people were there. Long story short, I was finally at the top of the hill and witnessed the remarkable view of the golden sunrise. I spent around 1 hour there just enjoying the breathtaking view which the nature offered.

After that, I went to Savannah Hill also known as the Telletubbies Hill of Bromo by the same jeep. Again, the view was just so green and bizarre. It felt peaceful in there. Then, the jeep took me to the Whispering Sands. It may have been only the sea of sands, but the atmosphere there was so surreal. I felt like I was visiting the different planet. The next destination was the Bromo Crater itself. The jeep dropped me off at the jeep parking area around 1 to 2 km from the crater. I could either walk or ride a horse to get to the crater. I chose walking instead, because the crater was on the top of the mountain and I felt bad seeing the horses taking people on their back while climbing. It was actually an easy climbing. It only took less than 30 minutes to the top. On the top of the mountain, it was not the crater that stole my attention, but the scenery. Everyone and everything looked so tiny from there. I believe that if you’d been there, you’d have said something philosophical like, “human was only a speck of dust in the universe”

It was great experience, but it’d have been greater and more fun if you’d been there. That’s all I can tell you about my holiday. I’ll tell you the rest another time. Now that I’ve shared mine, why don’t you share yours?

I hope you write me back soon.

Love,

Karen

Personal Letter

JAWABAN:

Dear Ameera,

How are you, sweetie? I'm writing because I miss you so much and I want to tell you about my holiday in Banda Aceh. And I know that you miss me because I don't write to you after a long time. I'm so sorry. You know that I'm very busy during my school day.

You know I spent my holiday in Banda Aceh with my family. Let me tell you about it. I spent my holiday in banda Aceh for three days. I went to some tourism places in Banda Aceh. On the first day, I went to Grand Mosque Baiturrahman. It is the most beautiful mosque that I have ever seen. It has seven domes and twelve electric umbrellas looks so fascinating especially in the night. You know? I was very happy because I could pray there. Since I wanted to watch the sunset so I decided to go to Ule Lhee. After that I and my family decided to take a rest at a guest house and would go to the Lampuuk beach at the dawn because we wanted to watch the sunrise. We also had lunch there. After we had lunch, we decided to go the Pucok Krueng. Oh you know, I was very tired but very happy. In the evening we drank coffee at Ule Kareng. The next morning, we had jogging at Blang Padang and we had breakfast there, too. After that we decided to go to Pasar Aceh for shopping. There was many beautiful merchandise and I bought some for you. Insha Allah I would send it when I had went back to Kuala Simpang.

It was great experience, but it’d have been greater and more fun if you’d been there. That’s all I can tell you about my holiday. I’ll tell you the rest another time. Now that I’ve shared mine, why don’t you share yours?

I hope you write me back soon.

Love,

Arsyla

The Meaning:

Ameera yang tersayang,

Bagaimana kabarmu sayang? Aku menulis surat karena aku sangat merindukanmu dan aku ingin memberi tahu mu tentang liburan ku di Banda Aceh. Dan aku tahu bahwa kamu merindukan ku karena aku tidak menulis surat kepada mu setelah waktu yang lama. aku minta maaf. Kamu tahu kan bahwa aku sangat sibuk selama hari sekolah.

Kamu tahu aku menghabiskan liburan ku di Banda Aceh bersama keluarga ku. Izinkan aku memberi tahu mu tentang hal itu. Aku menghabiskan liburan  ku di banda Aceh selama tiga hari. Aku pergi ke beberapa tempat wisata di Banda Aceh. Pada hari pertama, Aku pergi ke Masjid Agung Baiturrahman. Ini adalah masjid terindah yang pernah ku lihat. Ini memiliki tujuh kubah dan dua belas payung listrik terlihat begitu menarik terutama di malam hari. Kamu tahu? Aku sangat senang karena aku bisa sholat di sana. Karena aku ingin melihat matahari terbenam, maka aku memutuskan untuk pergi ke Ule Lhee. Setelah itu aku dan keluarga ku memutuskan untuk beristirahat di wisma dan akan pergi ke pantai Lampuuk saat fajar karena kami ingin menyaksikan matahari terbit. Kami juga makan siang di sana. Setelah makan siang, kami memutuskan untuk pergi ke Pucok Krueng. Oh, kamu tahu, aku sangat lelah tetapi sangat bahagia. Di malam hari kami minum kopi di Ule Kareng. Pagi berikutnya, kami jogging di Blang Padang dan kami juga sarapan di sana. Setelah itu kami memutuskan untuk pergi ke Pasar Aceh untuk berbelanja. Ada banyak barang dagangan yang indah dan saya membeli beberapa untuk mu. Inshaa Allah aku akan mengirimkannya untuk mu ketika aku kembali ke Kuala Simpang.

Itu pengalaman yang luar biasa, tetapi lebih besar dan lebih menyenangkan jika kamu ada di sana. Hanya itu yang bisa aku ceritakan tentang liburan ku. Aku akan memberitahumu sisanya di lain waktu. Sekarang, setelah aku membagikan tentang ku, mengapa kamu tidak membagikan tentang mu?

Aku harap kamu segera membalas ku.

Cinta,

Arsyla

29. surat bahasa inggris tentang liburan​


Jawaban:

Dear Regina,

How are you? I hope you’re fine. I’m writing this letter to tell you that I really miss you. Do you miss me too? It’s too bad that we couldn’t spend the last holiday together. I spent my holiday in Malang. I visited some places in the city, but among those places, Mount Bromo left the greatest impression on me.

Let me tell you how my tour in Mount Bromo went. I spent about 3 hours to get to Mount Bromo from Malang by public transportations. I arrived at the location in the afternoon. Since I wanted to watch the sunrise from the top of Penanjakan Hill which was one of the best sunrise spot in Mount Bromo, I decided to take a rest at a guest house and would go to the hill at the dawn. I woke up at 3 in the morning and as soon as I was ready, I rent a jeep to get to Penanjakan Hill. When arriving at the hill, many people were there. Long story short, I was finally at the top of the hill and witnessed the remarkable view of the golden sunrise. I spent around 1 hour there just enjoying the breathtaking view which the nature offered.

After that, I went to Savannah Hill also known as the Telletubbies Hill of Bromo by the same jeep. Again, the view was just so green and bizarre. It felt peaceful in there. Then, the jeep took me to the Whispering Sands. It may have been only the sea of sands, but the atmosphere there was so surreal. I felt like I was visiting the different planet. The next destination was the Bromo Crater itself. The jeep dropped me off at the jeep parking area around 1 to 2 km from the crater. I could either walk or ride a horse to get to the crater. I chose walking instead, because the crater was on the top of the mountain and I felt bad seeing the horses taking people on their back while climbing. It was actually an easy climbing. It only took less than 30 minutes to the top. On the top of the mountain, it was not the crater that stole my attention, but the scenery. Everyone and everything looked so tiny from there. I believe that if you’d been there, you’d have said something philosophical like, “human was only a speck of dust in the universe”

It was great experience, but it’d have been greater and more fun if you’d been there. That’s all I can tell you about my holiday. I’ll tell you the rest another time. Now that I’ve shared mine, why don’t you share yours?

I hope you write me back soon.

Love,

Karen
