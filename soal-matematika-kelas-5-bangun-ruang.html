50 hari = 15 tukang
50 - 20 - 5 = x tukang
[tex] \frac{50}{25} [/tex] = [tex] \frac{x}{15} [/tex] 
x = 2 × 15
x = 30

tambahan pekerja = 30 - 15 = 15
