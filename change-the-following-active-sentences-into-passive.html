2). Simple future

3). Simple past

10. Change the active sentences into passive.


Jawaban:

Passive voice

any pocket money is'nt given me by my parents.

Penjelasan:

that Passive voice, is used simple present Passive.

you, can look the pattern of simple present Passive and active.

______

pattern of simple present active

(+) S(he/she/it*singular person*) + verb 1 + s/es/ies + O/C

or

(+) S(I/you/they/we*plural person*) + verb 1 + O/C

(-) S(he/she/it) + does + not + verb 1 + O/C

or

(-) S(I/you/they/we) + do + not + verb 1 + O/C

(?) does + S(he/she/it) + verb 1 + O/C?

or

(?) do + S(I/you/they/we) + verb 1 + O/C?.

pattern of simple present Passive

(+) O + is/am/are + verb 3 ( past participle ) + by + S

(-) O + is/am/are + not + verb 3 + by + S

(?) is/am/are + O + verb 3 + by + S?..

____

NOTE!

is for subject singular person, (he/she/it) or person name, like "Siti"am for subject Iare for subject plural person, (they/we/you) like, "siti and Udin" or "you and me"

_____

*passive voice is used when the agent (S) is unknown.

*in Passive voice, object become subject, and subject become object

_____

14. change the following active sentences into passive ones​


Jawaban:

1. Anneke won a national robotic competition.

Passive: A national robotic competition was won by Anneke.

2. My new camera can capture images very well.

Passive: Images can be captured very well by my new camera.

3. The school holds a blog competition annually.

Passive: A blog competition is held by the school annually.

4. The company will launch a new product tomorrow.

Passive: A new product will be launched by the company tomorrow.

5. Visitors may not touch the articles in the museum.

Passive: The articles may not be touched by visitors in the museum.

6. My father was repairing my computer when the blackout occurred.

Passive: My computer was being repaired by my father when the blackout occurred.

7. Job seekers are attending a job fair at the town expo center.

Passive: A job fair at the town expo center is being attended by job seekers.

8. The multimedia students of my school have released a cartoon movie.

Passive: A cartoon movie has been released by the multimedia students of my school.

9. An international company will open a recruitment for bachelor degree.

Passive: A recruitment for bachelor degree will be opened by an international company

10. The rescue team prohibited the refugees of Mount Sinabung eruption to visit their houses.

Passive: The refugees of Mount Sinabung eruption was prohibited by the rescue team to visit their house.

Penjelasan:

1 dan 10. Passive past tense:

Object + was/were + verb 3 + by subject.

2 dan 5. Passive modals can/may:

Object + can/may + be + verb 3 + by subject.

3. Passive simple present tense:

Object + am/is/are + verb 3 + by subject.

4 dan 9. Passive simple future tense:

object + will + be + verb 3 + by subject.

6. Passive past continuous tense:

Object + was/were + being + verb 3 + by subject.

7. Passive present continuous tense:

Object + am/is/are + being + verb 3 + by subject.

8. Passive present perfect tense:

Object + have/has + been + verb 3 + by subject.

19. change the following active sentences into passive: vita founds wallet on the street


~ Answer:Wallet is founded by Vita on the street.

.

~ Discussion:

Halo dan salam kenal. Sebelum kita memindahkan kalimat aktif ke atas ke dalam bentuk pasif, kita perlu memerhatikan kalimat aktifnya dulu ya.

》Vita founds wallet on the street.kalimat di atas dinyatakan dalam bentuk simple present bahwa pekerjaan tersebut dilakukan sederhana saat ini. Dengan kata kerja dasar dengan sufiks -s/es untuk beberapa subyek tertentu.

Kalimat pasif berlangsung inaktif dimana subyek sebagai pemeran utama berubah menjadi penderita (agent). Untuk merubahnya:

nyatakan subyek sebagai obyeknyatakan obyek sebagai subyeknyatakan verba ke dalam bentuk past participlelengkapi dengan auxiliary/modal dan by sebelum subyek

Dalam simple present, kita menggunakan to be seperti is/am/are.

O + is/am/are + V3 + by — S + ...

to be dipilih salah satu menurut obyek.

is: he, she, it, name, objectam: Iare: you, they, we, names, objects

Ketentuan perubahan:

Found adalah regular verb jadi dapat menggunakan sufiks -ed. Nyatakan menjadi founded.Wallet adalah object sehingga sepakat menggunakan to be: is.On the street adalah complement. Kita dapat tetap nyatakan pada akhir kalimat.

JAWABANNYA:

Wallet is founded by Vita on the street.

30. Change the following sentences from active into passive she sings a song


Jawaban:

A song is sung by her

PEMBAHASAN

she sings a song

Structure Kalimatnya:Subject => she Verb+s => sings Object => a song

Berdasarkan keterangan di atas:Kalimat bentuk Present Tense karena setelah Subjek (she) menggunakan Verb+s (sings)Kalimat Aktif, karena Subjek melakukan aksi/tindakan dalam kalimatnya Kalimat Pasif. Yaitu Objek dikenai akso/tindakan dalam kalimatnya

Maka Rumus yg digunakan:

Active: S + v1 (-s/es) + O

=> she sings a song

Passive: O + Be (is/am/are) + By S

=> A song is sung by her
