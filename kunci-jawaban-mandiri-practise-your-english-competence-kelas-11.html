6. he comparative and superlative form of these adjectivesAdjectiveComparative Adjective Superlative Aeasyjuicyfriendlymodernfamouscarefullcarelessactiveimportantintellegentinterestingdiliciouslandiri Practise Your English Competence untuk SMP/MTs Kelas VII​


Jawaban:

Word - Comparative - Superlative

Easy - Easier - Easiest

juicy - juicier - juiciest

friendly - friendlier - friendliest

modern - more modern - most modern

famous - more famous - most famous

careful - more careful - most careful

careless - more careless - most careless

active - more active - most active

important - more important - most important

intellegent - more intelligent - most intelligent

interesting - more interesting - most interesting

delicious - more delicious - most delicious

Tips:

Ada beberapa kata yang bisa ditambahin -ier untuk comparative dan -iest untuk superlative, tapi kalau ga bisa, tinggal tambahin kata depan "more" buat comparative dan "most" buat superlative
