10. soal recount text pilihan ganda dan jawabannya


Berikut adalah contoh soal tentang recount text dalam bentuk pilihan ganda. Jawaban yang benar adalah jawaban yang digarisbawahi.

Perhatikan bacaan berikut ini!

When Claire visited the Statue of Liberty for the first time, she instantly admired it as a symbol of freedom. Claire made sure to make reservations before her visit because only 240 people are permitted to climb the staircase to the top of the statue every day. After climbing almost 400 stairs, Claire received spectacular views of the city from the statue’s crown.

During her visit, Claire learned that the Statue of Liberty was not always the color that it is now. She found out that because the statue’s exterior is made of copper, the statue oxidized over time, giving it the greenish appearance it has in present day. When it was first constructed, the statue was the same color as a shiny penny!

After touring the Statue of Liberty, Claire spent the rest of the day in New York City visiting other important monuments and historic landmarks. Claire left New York hoping to have had the time to explore more sites, but she can’t wait to return to the city in the future.

1. How many people are permitted to climb the Statue of Liberty's stairs per day?

A. 100
B. 150
C. 200
D. 240

2. From which part of the Statue of Liberty did Claire get spectacular views of the city?

A. The eyes
B. The hands
C. The crown
D. The shoulder

3. What makes the Statue of Liberty changed colour over time?

A. Because the statue is poorly maintained.
B. One restoration painted the statue green.
C. It got covered by air pollution.
D. The copper exterior of the statue oxidized.

4. The text above is written in the form of a ... text.

A. recount
B. narrative
C. descriptive
D. report

Pembahasan

Recount text adalah salah satu jenis teks dalam Bahasa Inggris yang bercerita tentang kegiatan atau tindakan yang dilakukan oleh tokoh-tokoh dalam cerita tersebut. Biasanya, recount text ditulis dengan tujuan untuk memberikan informasi sekaligus menghibur pembaca.

Recount text memiliki struktur teks sebagai berikut:

Orientation/orientasi, yaitu bagian teks yang berisi informasi tentang tokoh dan latar belakang kejadian.Events, yaitu bagian teks yang berisi cerita tentang pengalaman yang ingin diceritakan oleh penulis.Reorientation, yaitu bagian teks yang berisi kesimpulan atau rangkuman mengenai apa yang telah diceritakan oleh penulis.

A Trip to the Zoo

Yesterday my family and I went to the zoo.  It was a big zoo in our city. We left home at about 9 in the morning. It was one hour away driving.

When we got at the zoo, it was still not many visitors, we went to the shop to buy some food to give to the animals.  After getting the food we went to the nocturnal house where we saw birds and reptiles which only come out at night.  There were a lot of animals: elephants, tigers, lions, meerkat and etc.

Before lunch we went for a ride on the elephant. It was a thrill to ride it. My little brother was scared at first but he enjoyed it eventually. During lunch we fed some birds in the park. In the afternoon we saw the animals being fed by the zookeeper.

When we returned home we were all tired but happy because we had so much fun. We arrived home at about 4 o'clock.

Penjelasan:

Recount Text adalah suatu teks yang ditulis untuk menceritakan kembali kejadian atau peristiwa yang sudah terjadi di waktu lampau.

Tujuan dari recount text adalah untuk memberi informasi atau menghibur pembacanya. Untuk menyusun recount text, kalimat yang ditulis menggunakan tenses berikut ini:

Simple Past Tense

Past Continuous Tense

Past Perfect Tense

Generic Structure dari recount text adalah:

Orientation: pada bagian ini menyebutkan siapa saja yang terlibat dalam cerita, kapan dan dimana cerita tersebut terjadiEvents: menceritakan kembali secara kronologisRe-orientation: penutup cerita atau

27. contoh soal recount text dan pembahasannya


Contoh soal recount text dan pembahasannya:

1. What is recount text?

Answer: A recount text is a text to retell a past experience.

2. What is the purpose of recount text?

Answer: The purpose of recount text is to informing or entertaining the reader

3. Mention the generic status of recount text!

Answer: The generic status of recount text:

Orientation: this part is to inform who involve in the story, where and when the story happenedEvents: the story in the chronological sequenceRe-orientation: ending or closing

4. What tenses do we use in recount text?

Answer: Because recount text is about an experience in the past so the tenses used in the story are Simple Past Tense, Past Continuous Tense or Past Perfect Tense.
