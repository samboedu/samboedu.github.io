6. SENTENCES INTO GOOD ENGLISH WITH THE COMPLETE FORM (+), (-), (?)! 1. TEMAN SAYA SANGAT PINTAR 2. IBU SAYA SANGAT CANTIK 3. MUHAMMAD SANGAT RAMAH 4. SELURUH SISWA SEDANG BERADA DIKELAS SEKARANG 5. BUKU INI SANGAT MAHAL 6. RUMAH KU SANGAT JAUH DARI SEKOLAH KU 7. BAHASA INGGRIS SANGAT PENTING SEKARANG


Jawaban:

1. (+) My friend is very clever.

(-) My friend isn't very clever.

(?) Is my friend very clever?

2. (+) My mother is very beautiful.

(-) My mother isn't very beautiful.

(?) Is my mother very beautiful?

3. (+) Muhammad is very friendly.

(-) Muhammad isn't very friendly.

(?) Is Muhammad very friendly?

4. (+) All of students are in the class now.

(-) All of students aren't in the class now.

(?) Are all the students in the class now?

5. (+) This book is very expensive.

(-) This book isn't very expensive.

(?) Is this book very expensive?

6. (+) My house is very far from my school.

(-) My house isn't very far from my school.

(?) Is my house very far from my school?

7. (+) English is very important now.

(-) English isn't very important now.

(?) Is English very important now?

Penjelasan:

Kalimat di atas menggunakan simple present tense berbentuk nominal, atau tidak membutuhkan kata kerja.

Rumus nominal simple present tense adalah

(+) Subject + am/is/are + noun/adverb/adjective.

(-) Subject + am/is/are + not + noun/adverb/adjective.

(?) Am/is/are + subject + noun/adverb/adjective?

17. jawaban buku bahasa inggris kelas 12 halaman 6-7 k-13


Buku bahasa Inggris kelas 12 halaman 6-7 adalah mengenai offering help or services. Pada materi offering help or service ini disajikan 4 dialog yang kemudian diberikan pertanyaan yang berhubungan dengan dialog-dialog tersebut.

Pembahasan

1. Where do you think each conversation takes place?

Dialog 1: in a doctor’s room.

Dialog 2: in a bus station.

Dialog 3: at school.

Dialog 4: at home

2. What are the relationships between the speakers?

Dialog 1: doctor and patient.

Dialog 2: tourist and ticket seller.

Dialog 3: friends.

Dialog 4: friends.

3. What are the functions of the underlined words?

Responses of expressions of offering of help/services.

4. What are the functions of the italicized words?

Expressions of offering help/services.

5. In Dialog 1, what does dr. Nahda say to help Fafa?

Dr. Nahda says, ‘What can I do for you?’, ‘What’s the problem?’ and ‘okay, let me check your stomach‘.

What will dr. Nahda do to help Fafa?

He will check Fafa’s stomach.

6. Look at Dialog 2. What does Tania offer to the stranger?

She offers a bus ticket indirectly.

Does the stranger accept Tania’s offer?

Yes. The stranger accepts Tania’s offer by saying,

What does he say?

"Yes. I need to go to Jakarta, …… "

"Thank you. I will buy the bus ticket, then."

7. Who is offering a help in Dialog 3?

In Dialog 3, Dhea is offering a help.

What does she say?

She says, "Would you need my help?"

Is the offer accepted?

No, it is not. The offer is not accepted (No, thanks. I’ll do it as soon as possible).

8. In Dialog 4, what does Diana say to offer a help?

Diana says, "What if I help you with the preparation?"

Does Hamada accept or refuse the help? What does she say?

Hamada refuses the offer politely by saying, "Oh, it’s a very nice of you. But I'm going to do it with my sister. Thanks for the offer."

9. Write the patterns of offering help/services.

May I help you…?Would you like ...?What if I ...?)

10. Write possible responses for offering help/services.

- Acceptance:

Yes, I need …Yes, I’d love to …Thanks a lot.

- Refusing:

No, thank you …Yes, but ...Thanks a lot, but ...Pelajari lebih lanjut
